**Possible Diseases Causing Fatigue, Vomiting, and Loose Motions in a 20-Year-Old Female with History of Diabetes and Recent Travel to Paris**

* **Influenza (Flu)**: A respiratory viral infection that can cause systemic symptoms, including fatigue, vomiting, and diarrhea.

**Other Conditions:**

* **Diabetic Ketoacidosis (DKA)**: A life-threatening complication of diabetes characterized by high blood sugar, electrolyte imbalances, and intense thirst, nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain.

* **Appendicitis**: Inflammation of the appendix, leading to intense abdominal pain, nausea, and potentially vomiting.
