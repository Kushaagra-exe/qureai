**Possible Diseases Causing Running Nose, Headache, Sore Throat, and Weakness in a Patient with a History of Nose Surgery and Potential Tuberculosis Exposure:**

**1. Common Cold:**

* Caused by viruses
* Symptoms: runny nose, sore throat, headache, weakness, sneezing

**2. Influenza (Flu):**

* Caused by influenza viruses
* Symptoms: similar to common cold, but can be more severe with fever, muscle aches, and fatigue

**3. Sinusitis:**

* Inflammation of the sinuses
* Symptoms: nasal congestion, runny nose, facial pain, headache

**4. Tuberculosis:**

* Bacterial infection that primarily affects the lungs
* Symptoms: cough, weight loss, fever, night sweats, chest pain
* **Important Note:** Tuberculosis is a serious disease and requires prompt medical attention.

**5. Meningitis:**

* Inflammation of the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord
* Symptoms: severe headache, fever, nausea, vomiting, sensitivity to light

**6. Encephalitis:**

* Inflammation of the brain
* Symptoms: headache, fever, confusion, seizures

**7. Post-Nasal Drip:**

* Mucus from the nose drains down the back of the throat
* Symptoms: sore throat, cough, nasal congestion

**8. Allergies:**

* Immune system reaction to substances like pollen, dust, or pet dander
* Symptoms: runny nose, sneezing, itchy eyes, congestion

**9. Other Respiratory Infections:**

* Pneumonia
* Bronchitis
* Laryngitis

**10. Non-Infectious Conditions:**

* Sinus headaches
* Tension headaches
* Migraines
